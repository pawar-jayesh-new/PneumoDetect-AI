Who We Are

About PneumoDetect

PneumoDetect is an AI-based healthcare platform developed to detect Pneumonia using advanced deep learning algorithms and chest X-ray analysis. Our goal is to provide fast, accurate, and accessible medical support through intelligent technology.

Our Mission

To make early pneumonia detection faster and more reliable using AI.

Our Vision

To bridge the gap between healthcare and artificial intelligence.

Our Technology

Deep Learning models trained on thousands of medical X-ray images.

Process

How PneumoDetect Works

Step 1 – Upload X-ray

User uploads chest X-ray image into the system.

Step 2 – AI Processing

Deep learning model analyzes the image using trained CNN algorithms.

Step 3 – Disease Classification

The system classifies whether the X-ray shows Pneumonia as Normal, Viral, or Bacterial.

Step 4 – Instant Result

User receives fast and accurate prediction results instantly.
